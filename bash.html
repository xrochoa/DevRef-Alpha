Permission modes for chmod - Write/Read/Execute for User/Group/Other

Alpha notation ugo=rwx, u=rwx, g=rw, o=r, ug+w (add w), o-w(removes w), a+rxw (all for all)
Octal notation r=4, w=2, x=1, 777 (all for all), 700 (all for me, none for them), 755 (all for me, rx for them)
